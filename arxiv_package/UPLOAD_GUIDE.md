# arXiv Upload Guide

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Before You Start

1. **Create an arXiv account** at https://arxiv.org/
   - Use your institutional email if possible
   - arXiv requires email verification
   - Approval for category submissions requires endorsement (see below)

2. **Read arXiv policies**:
   - https://arxiv.org/help/license/faq
   - https://arxiv.org/help/endorsed
   - https://arxiv.org/help/subject_classification

### Step 1: Prepare Your Package

Your arxiv_package directory contains:
- `main.tex` - Source files (arXiv will compile)
- `references.bib` - Bibliography
- `main.pdf` - Optional PDF preview

### Step 2: Submit via arXiv Website

1. **Go to** https://arxiv.org/
2. **Click "Contribute to arXiv"**
3. **Sign in** with your arXiv credentials

### Step 3: Choose Your Submission Path

You'll need to select:
- "I am uploading for the first time" OR
- "I've previously uploaded"

### Step 4: Upload Files

1. **Click "Upload files" or drag and drop:
   - `main.tex` (required)
   - `references.bib` (recommended)
   - You can upload the entire `arxiv_package` folder

### Step 6: License Agreement

1. Read the arXiv license agreement
2. Check the checkbox to agree
3. Submit

### Step 7: Endorsement (If First Time)

If this is your first submission, you need endorsement from an arXiv-endorsed author. Find an arXiv-endorsed author who can endorse you (typically a collaborator or someone familiar with your work)

### Step 8: Submit for Approval

1. Review your submission
2. Click "Submit" or "Confirm"
3. Wait for arXiv approval (typically 1 business day)

## Post-Submission

### arXiv ID Assignment

1. **Check your email** for arXiv confirmation
2. **Get your arXiv ID** (e.g., arXiv:2603.xxxxx)

### After Approval

1. arXiv will email you your arXiv ID
2. You'll get a link to your paper on arXiv
3. Share your arXiv link with collaborators

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: "Not endorsed for cs.AI"
**Solution:** Find an arXiv-endorsed author to endorse you

### Issue: "Compilation failed"
**Solution:** arXiv will email you the log file - fix the issue and resubmit

### Issue: "License not compatible"
**Solution:** arXiv requires arXiv-nonexclusive license
